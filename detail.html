Now that we know the precautions for hiking in winter, let's find out where to go I recommend Sanbangjangsan Mountain, which is cool in summer and drunk with beautiful scenery in winter! It is a mountain on the border between Jeongeup-si, Gochang-gun, Jeollabuk-do and Buk-myeon, Jangseong-gun, Jeollanam-do. At first, it was renamed Bangjangsan Mountain, meaning "the mountain is wide and big, so it covers the people." It is called Samsinsan Mountain, including Jirisan Mountain and Mudeungsan Mountain.

Bangjangsan Mountain has a natural recreation forest managed by the National Recreation Forest Management Office. The trees here are more beautiful when snow piles up, making it a winter forest attraction. In addition, Bangjangsan Mountain has a forest road that can be passed by car from the recreational forest to paragliding, so it is good to enjoy winter sports paragliding as well as hiking in winter.
